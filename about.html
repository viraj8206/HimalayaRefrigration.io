About

Welcome to Himalaya Refrigeration Kalawad

Himalaya Refrigeration kalawad has been working on repairing your fridge, washing machine, Automatic Domestic Flourmill since 1985.

"Himalayan Refrigeration Kalawad Repairing provides services for all aspects of refrigeration and piping including maintenance of existing facilities."

As part of this, the Himalayas that serve thousands of homes and commercial buildings, provide people with comfort and peace of mind at home or at work, save energy, reduce costs and provide state-of-the-art technology for industry - Himalayan Refrigeration Products All of our Himalayan products provide important knowledge base for temperature control technology to affiliates and customers.

At Himalaya Refrigeration we acknowledge the need to constantly research and evaluate new technologies and rearrange them according to the needs of our customers where applicable. Technological excellence is only one part of the service because the formation of strong customer partnerships enables us to fully understand and respond to their ongoing needs.

“Quality - is not an act, it's a habit”
